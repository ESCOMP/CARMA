This routine evaluates particle loss rates due to nucleation : heterogeneous nucleation of glassy aerosols only,.

The parameterization of glass aerosols is described in Murray et al. [Nature Geosciences, 2010], and is based upon measurements of the nucleation of citric acid aerosols at cold temperatures.

NOTE: This implementation assumes that the aerosol being nucleated is the total aerosol population and not just the fraction of aerosols that are glassy. To account for homogenous freezing of the aerosol population, the routine freezaerl also needs to be called and the overall nucleation rate is the sum of the rates for homogeneous freezing and for heterogenous nucleation.

The parameter fglass is the fraction of the total aerosol population that will be in a glassy state for T <= 212K.

The loss rates for all particle elements in a particle group are equal.

Author: Chuck Bardeen, Eric Jensen

Version: Apr-2010
