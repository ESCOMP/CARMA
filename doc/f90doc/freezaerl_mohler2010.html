This routine evaluates particle loss rates due to nucleation : aerosol freezing only.

The parameterization described by Mohler et al., presented at the AMS Cloud physics workshop (2010) is used.

The loss rates for all particle elements in a particle group are equal.

To avoid nucleation into an evaporating bin, this subroutine must be called after growp, which evaluates evaporation loss rates .

Author: Chuck Bardeen

Version: Aug-2010
