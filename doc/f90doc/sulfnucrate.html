Calculates particle production rates due to nucleation : binary homogeneous nucleation of sulfuric acid and water only Numerical method follows one of the following: 1. Zhao & Turco, JAS, V.26, No.5, 1995. 2. Vehkamaki, H., M. Kulmala, I. Napari, K.E.J. Lehtinen, C. Timmreck, M. Noppel and A. Laaksonen, 2002, An improved parameterization for sulfuric acid-water nucleation rates for tropospheric and stratospheric conditions, J. Geophys. Res., 107, 4622, doi:10.1029/2002jd002184

Author: Mike Mills, Chuck Bardeen

Version: May-2022
