Calculates the vapor pressure for sulfuric acid.

and are vapor pressures in units of [dyne/cm^2]

Created Dec-1995 (Ackerman) Modified Sep-1997 (McKie) Modified Jul-2001 (Mills)

NOTE: To calculate vapor pressure of H2SO4 water vapor pressure (pvapl(iz, igash2o)) should be calculated before this calculation.

Author: Mike Mills, Tianyi Fan
