This routine evaluates particle loss rates due to nucleation : aerosol freezing only.

The parameterization described by Tabazadeh et al. [GRL, 27, 1111, 2000.] is used.

The loss rates for all particle elements in a particle group are equal.

Author: Eric Jensen, Chuck Bardeen

Version: Mar-1995, Nov-2009
