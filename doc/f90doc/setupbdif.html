This routine evaluates particle vertical diffusion coefficients, dkz(k,i,j) [cm^2 s^-1].

Method: Uses equation 8.73 from Seinfeld and Pandis [1998] along with the slip correction factor (bpm) calculated in the fall velocity setup.

This routine requires that vertical profiles of temperature , air density , viscosity , and slip correction are defined (i.e., initatm.f and setupvf.f must be called before this).

NOTE: Eddy diffusion is carried out by the parent model, so the only diffusion that CARMA does is Brownian diffusion.
