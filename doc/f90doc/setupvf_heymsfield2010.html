This routine evaluates particle fall velocities, vf(k) [cm s^-1] and reynolds' numbers based on fall velocities, re(j,i,k) [dimensionless]. indices correspond to vertical level , bin index , and aerosol group .

Method: Use the routined from Heymsfield and Westbrook [2010], which is designed only for ice particles. Thus this routine uses the dry mass and radius, not the wet mass and radius. The area ration (Ar) is determined based upon the formulation of Schmitt and Heymsfield [JAS, 2009].
