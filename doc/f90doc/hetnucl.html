This routine evaluates particle loss rates due to nucleation : heterogeneous deposition nucleation only. The parameters are adjusted for mesospheric conditions, based upon the recommendations of Keesee.

Based on expressions from ... Keesee [JGR,1989] Pruppacher and Klett [2000] Rapp and Thomas [JASTP, 2006] Trainer et al. [2008]

The loss rates for all particle elements in a particle group are equal.

To avoid nucleation into an evaporating bin, this subroutine must be called after growp, which evaluates evaporation loss rates .

Author: Eric Jensen, Chuck Bardeen

Version: Oct-2000, Jan-2010
