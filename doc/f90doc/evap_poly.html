This routine calculates particle source terms due to total evaporation into a polydisperse CN distribution by assuming that the pdf of core mass is log-normal skewed by mass raised to the -3/2 power (which guarantees average core mass from pdf is the same as average core mass).

Distinct evaporation of cores has not been treated.

Author: Andy Ackerman

Version: Aug-2001
